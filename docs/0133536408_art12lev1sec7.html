M12.7 Summary

Automation and control in biomedicine is an important area of research, with many potential long-term health care benefits. Sensors, the difficulty of developing a reliable model, and "fuzzy" performance specifications all create difficulties in feedback-control design. For each of the examples presented in this module, it is recommended that you perform a literature review to understand the current state of the art.
