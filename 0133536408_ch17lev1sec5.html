3:

  1. Begin discussing, with the process operator, a recent theoretical article from the International Journal of Unapplied Incomprehensible Control.   2. Grab a cup of coffee and sit down and discuss the most recent local high school football game with the process operator. After a while, begin to direct the conversation to the subject of the catalytic reforming process and discuss possible operating changes that have occurred during the past few weeks.
